XENIUM

Xenium is the Annual Technical event conducted by Parikalan-The Computer Science Society of P.G.D.A.V.(M) College. It encompasses all the spheres of IT and gives a platform to faster the brilliance inside these talented students.
Each and every member strives to create magic with their skills and thus we have our fest captioned "Coding Logic, Creating Magic". And as we hit the stage with the most awaited brain bursting technical events, we promise a launch pad for the students with technical knowledge, like never before!
